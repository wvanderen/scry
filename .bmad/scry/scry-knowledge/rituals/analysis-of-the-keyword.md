## Structured & Enhanced Version

### Understanding the Symbolism

**The Light at the Top of the Universe**
- Visualize energy of heaven as brilliant white light above
- Colors can be modified based on correspondences to the Tree of Life, elements, planets
- The LVX Signs are powerful occult metaphors for light (L=Isis, V=Apophis, X=Osiris Risen)

**Energy Work Foundation**
- This rite is a form of directed energy work
- Visualization and gestures aid in summoning and directing the energy
- The three-part gesture sequence builds and releases the charge

### Preparation Steps

**Standard Preliminaries**
1. Perform Standard Preliminaries for Ritual Work
2. Clear and prepare your space
3. Have target object/intention clearly in mind

**Recommended Opening Sequence**
1. **LBRP** (Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram) - Clear space
2. **LIRP** (Lesser Invoking Ritual of the Pentagram) - Call general force
3. **BRH/IRH or GIRP/GIRH** - Tune to specific energy type

**Selecting Energy Type**
- Choose element/planet/sephira based on your working
- Perform corresponding invoking rituals before the Keyword

**Duration:** Total preparation: 10-20 minutes

## The Core Ritual

### Sign of Isis (L)
1. Stand upright, arms raised in a V-shape above head
2. Visualize brilliant white light at the top of the universe
3. **Inhale deeply** - see the light growing brighter and brighter
4. Hold the gesture as you breathe in fully
5. **Exhale** - transition smoothly to next sign

### Sign of Apophis (V)
1. Arms extended horizontally to sides forming a cross
2. Head tilted back slightly
3. Focus again on the white light at the top of the universe
4. **Inhale deeply** - the light intensifies further
5. Hold the gesture during the full inhalation
6. **Exhale** - transition to final sign

### Sign of Osiris Risen (X)
1. Arms crossed over chest (right over left)
2. Head bowed slightly forward
3. Focus on the white light at the top of the universe
4. **Inhale deeply** - light becomes as bright as possible
5. Hold the breath and gesture
6. **Vibrate/Speak clearly:**

   **"LET THE DIVINE LIGHT DESCEND"**

7. **On the vibration:** See a powerful, dense shaft of light descend from the top of the universe down into your space/target
8. Watch the light completely fill the room or target object with brilliant energy

**Duration:** 3-5 minutes for core rite

## Variations and Advanced Techniques

### Color Correspondences

**Black - Saturn/Binah**
- Binding, protection, banishing
- Binding formula: "In the divine name **YHVH Elohim** I ask that this person be bound so they cause me no harm and be banished from my life in a way that brings harm to none and is for the good of all and in no way let this reverse or bring me or them any curse"

**Green - Venus/Netzach**
- Wealth, pleasure, enthusiasm, joy, life, money

**Yellow - Sun/Tiphareth**
- Radiance, power, health, divinity, individuality

**Red - Mars/Geburah**
- Power, energy, smashing obstacles, destructive ability, clearing out the unneeded

**Reference:** Consult Liber 777 for comprehensive correspondences

- Mars/Geburah: **ELOHIM GIBOR**

### Remote Working

You can call down the light to places where you are not physically present:
- Focus on the person's energy/essence rather than physical location
- Can be used for healing or binding from a distance
- Visualization of the person/place is sufficient

### Repeated Workings for Power

**40-Day Cycle (Spiritual Year)**
- For major spells, magical items, or tinctures
- Charge the object daily for 40 days
- Builds tremendous power through repetition
- Not required for all workings - use intuition

**Progressive Mastery**
- Practice daily or twice daily
- Energy becomes stronger over time
- Rite becomes easier with repetition
- Eventually becomes second nature

## Important Warnings and Safety

### Protecting Charged Objects

**CRITICAL:** Do NOT perform the LBRP around items you have charged with this ritual unless:
- They are put away in a closed container
- Covered with a cloth
- Removed from the space

**Reason:** The LBRP will deconsecrate charged items and undo your work.

### Grounding After

- Return to normal awareness slowly
- Ground any excess energy
- Record your experience while fresh
