# Clauneck Ritual Procedures

## Core Evocation Protocol

### Preparation Requirements
- Clean altar with appropriate purification
- Timing: Tuesday during daylight hours preferred
- Formal ritual attire and mental preparation
- All materials prepared before invocation

### Traditional Invocation
- Use enn: "Ahvalen Esen Clauneck Kiar"
- Proper ceremonial opening and circle casting
- Display sigil prominently during working
- Offerings presented before petitions

### Sigil Protocol
- Use Grimorium Verum sigil as primary authority
- Render accurately on appropriate medium
- Focus intent during sigil activation
- Proper disposal or storage after working

## Offering Guidelines

### Traditional Offerings
- Alcohol: Wine, whiskey (quality matters)
- Food: Pork products, green apples
- Plants: Cacti, holly, specific herbs
- Materials: Candles (green, red), money
- Personal: Blood (if practitioner chooses), written proclamation

### Offering Protocol
- Present before making requests
- Explain significance of each offering
- Maintain respectful demeanor throughout
- Proper disposal after ritual completion

## Pact Construction

### Pact Requirements
- Clear articulation of both sides' obligations
- Signed in green ink with blood (if practitioner chooses)
- Witnesses or proper ceremonial witnessing
- Specific timelines and conditions

### Safety Protocols
- Ensure all terms are understood by both parties
- Include escape clauses and termination conditions
- Verify no harm comes to either party
- Document pact details discreetly

## Timing Considerations

### Optimal Timing
- Tuesday (day of Mars/War - appropriate for decisive action)
- Daylight hours (Clauneck considered a "day demon")
- Specific planetary hours if practitioner is knowledgeable
- Avoid conflicting astrological conditions

### Regional Variations
- Note practitioner's location and traditional adaptations
- Adjust for local customs and materials availability
- Consider seasonal variations in effectiveness
- Document what works in different contexts

## Correspondence Integration

### Color Coordination
- Primary: Purple (royalty, wealth), Red (power), Green (prosperity)
- Secondary: Gold (abundance), Black (authority)
- Use in candles, altar cloths, ritual clothing

### Material Selection
- Metals: Bronze, brass (traditional associations)
- Stones: Garnet, bloodstone, carnelian, ruby, clear quartz
- Herbs: Pennyroyal, mugwort, basil, cardamom, cinnamon, patchouli, mint, ginger, bay leaves
- Incense: Tobacco, dragon's blood
