## TEMPLE CONFIGURATION FOR THEOPHANY

### Optimal Setup
- **Triangle against wall** - Creates natural boundary between worlds
- **Obsidian disk center** - Primary chthonic manifestation portal
- **Crystal ball below** - Secondary viewing portal with different angles
- **Circle practitioner** - Primary conduit and anchor
- **Chair practitioner** - Observer and recorder
- **Three candles** - At triangle points, central one slightly taller

### Dual Scrying Synergy
- **Obsidian**: Primary portal for chthonic manifestation (darker, ancient aspect)
- **Crystal ball**: Secondary portal showing reflections/light patterns
- Both practitioners watch different surfaces but describe observations

## MANIFESTATION LEVELS

### Level 1: Scrying Surface Manifestation (Most Common)
- Obsidian shows moving shadows, shapes, or developing faces
- Crystal ball reflects corresponding light patterns or images
- Safest and most accessible form of theophany

### Level 2: Reflected Space Manifestation
- Appears between the two scrying surfaces
- Visible as air distortions, light patterns, heat shimmer
- Multiple reflections in the obsidian

### Level 3: Threshold Space Manifestation (Advanced)
- Appears to emerge from behind/within the triangle
- Partial visibility - torso or head in shadow/silhouette form
- Rare but profound when achieved

## RITUAL TECHNIQUES

### Focused Theophany Rite
**Timing:** Dusk or midnight, room dark except candles

**Preparation:**
- 10 minutes silent meditation together
- Salt water hand cleansing for both practitioners
- Candle lighting: bottom point first, then triangle points

**Invocation (Both Together):**
> "Hekate of the crossroads,
> Between the stone and crystal,
> Between the dark and light,
> Show yourself in sacred sight.
> Not in imagination,
> But in this sacred space.
> By key and torch and ancient rite,
> Manifest your holy light."

**Manifestation Technique:**
1. Circle practitioner: Focus on obsidian, hold key
2. Chair practitioner: Focus on crystal ball, document
3. Synchronized breathing: 3 in, hold 3, 3 out
4. After 5 minutes: Chant "Hekate, Triformis, Trivia, Phoebe"
5. Energy peak: Touch key to obsidian frame

## SIGNS OF APPROACHING THEOPHANY

### In Obsidian Disk
- Surface becomes deeper, darker, more liquid
- Shadows move independently of light source
- Faces/figures forming in depths
- Reflection changes to show other space

### In Crystal Ball
- Sudden mists or clouds forming
- Light patterns not matching candle positions
- Images corresponding to obsidian manifestations
- Same figure from different angles

### In Space Between
- Sudden temperature changes
- Air shimmers/distorts between surfaces
- Shadows appearing without physical sources
- Sound of keys turning or distant dogs
