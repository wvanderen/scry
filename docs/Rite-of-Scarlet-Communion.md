# THE RITE OF SCARLET COMMUNION
## A Two-Person Ritual for Invoking and Communing with Babalon

---

## ROLES
**Magus (Operator):** Leads ritual, handles invocations, manages energy flow
**Seer (Scryer):** Receives visions, works with crystal ball, speaks Babalon's words

---

## SETUP (Both prepare together)

**Right Altar (by Triangle):** White cloth, incense, blade, scarlet cord, white candle
**Left Altar:** Red cloth, cup of red wine, red roses, Babalon sigil, red candle
**Center:** Crystal ball on red cushion, surrounded by rose petals

**Positioning:** Magus stands between altars, Seer sits at back facing crystal/Triangle

---

## PHASE I: OPENING

**Magus:** Performs Qabalistic Cross, then LBRP to purify space

**Magus (proclaims):** *"I consecrate this temple as gateway between worlds. Let the veils grow thin, let the Scarlet Woman ride forth into this sacred space."*

---

## PHASE II: CONSECRATION

**Magus (at Right Altar):** Traces **unicursal invoking hexagram** before Triangle, vibrates *"ARARITA!"*

**Magus:** *"This Triangle becomes the throne of Babalon. By authority of True Will, I declare this Triangle consecrated to BABALON, the Scarlet Woman!"*

**Magus:** Places scarlet cord around Triangle perimeter

**Magus (at Left Altar):** Lifts cup of wine

**Magus:** *"This cup becomes the Grail of Babalon. Let this wine become the sacrament of the Scarlet Woman."*

**Magus:** Makes **invoking pentagram of Fire** over cup, vibrates *"BABALON!"*, returns it to altar

---

## PHASE III: THE GREAT INVOCATION

**Magus (before crystal, facing Triangle):** Arms raised, palms forward

**Magus (vibrates):** *"BABALON! BABALON! BABALON!"*

**Magus:** *"I call thee forth from the City of Pyramids! Come forth, thou Scarlet Woman, Lady of the Grail, Mother of Abominations!"*

**Magus:** *"I invoke thee by thy secret names: BABALON! By the power of IAO! By the formula of ARARITA!"*

**Magus:** *"Come forth into this Triangle of Arte! Appear unto us! Speak through the crystal! Fill this temple with thy terrible beauty!"*

**Magus:** *"OL SONUF VAORESAJI, BABALON! Come forth in power and manifestation!"*

**Magus (with passion):** *"BABALON, appear unto us! Manifest within this Triangle! Speak through the crystal! We are thy servants, thy willing chalices. Come unto us, now and ever more!"*

---

## PHASE IV: THE COMMUNION

**Magus (to Seer):** *"The way is open. Begin the scrying."*

**Seer:** Softens gaze upon crystal ball, enters light trance state

**Seer (speaks softly):** *"Great Babalon, we have called and thou hast answered. Show thyself in the crystal. Grant us vision, grant us communion."*

**Seer:** Gazes into crystal, receives visions/communications

**Magus:** Maintains sacred space, monitors energy flow, may softly chant *"Babalon"* if presence needs strengthening

**When communication is established:**

**Seer:** Speaks Babalon's words, shares visions, channels messages
**Magus:** May ask questions, make offerings, facilitate deeper communion

**Magus (when communion feels complete):** *"The communion is complete. Prepare for the sacrament."*

---

## PHASE V: THE SACRAMENT

**Magus:** *"This cup is the cup of BABALON. In it is mingled the blood of the saints. We drink, and thereby partake of Her essence."*

**Magus drinks first, then offers to Seer**

**Seer:** *"I have drunk from the Grail. I am transformed. The communion is complete."*

**Both:** Visualize crimson light filling entire bodies

---

## PHASE VI: SEALING

**Magus:** *"Great Babalon, we give thanks for thy presence, wisdom, and love. The veil between us has grown thin, and we have beheld thy glory."*

**Magus:** *"Remain ever accessible to us. Let this temple be a gateway. When we call, answer. When we seek, appear."*

**Magus:** *"Hail unto thee, BABALON! Io Babalon! Amor sub voluntate!"*

**Magus:** *"Depart now in peace, great Babalon, unto thy throne beyond the Abyss. We release thee from this Triangle. Go in peace, that thou mayest return when called. Ave atque vale!"*

---

## PHASE VII: CLOSING

**Magus:** Performs Qabalistic Cross

**Magus:** Performs LBRP to close space

**Magus:** Extinguishes candles in reverse order (Right, then Left)

**Magus:** *"The rite is ended. The temple is sealed. That which was opened is closed. Yet the connection remains. So it is done."*

---

## SYMBOL CLARIFICATIONS

### UNICURSAL HEXAGRAM (Triangle Consecration)
Draw in one continuous line:
1. Start at top point, draw down to lower right
2. Continue up to upper left
3. Across to upper right
4. Down to lower left
5. Across to bottom point
6. Back up to starting point
Vibrate: **"ARARITA"**

### INVOKING PENTAGRAM OF FIRE (Cup Consecration)
Draw as:
1. Start at top point, draw down to lower right corner
2. Up to upper left corner
3. Across to upper right corner
4. Down to lower left corner
5. Back up to starting point
Vibrate: **"BABALON"** or **"IAO"**

### LBRP PENTAGRAMS (Phase I & VII)
Use standard **Earth banishing pentagrams** at four quarters
